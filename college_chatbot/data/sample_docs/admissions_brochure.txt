SPRINGFIELD INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY - ADMISSIONS BROCHURE 2026-27

1. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech): Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics,
Chemistry, and Mathematics, securing a minimum of 60% aggregate marks (55% for
reserved categories). A valid rank in the state or national engineering entrance
examination is mandatory.

Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech/MBA): A relevant bachelor's degree with a minimum of
55% aggregate marks, along with a valid GATE/CAT/state PGCET score.

2. AVAILABLE COURSES AND DEPARTMENTS
- Department of Computer Science and Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
- Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
- Department of Mechanical Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
- Department of Civil Engineering (B.Tech)
- Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (B.Tech)
- School of Management Studies (MBA)

3. APPLICATION PROCEDURE
Step 1: Register on the online admission portal with a valid email and phone number.
Step 2: Fill in personal, academic, and entrance exam details.
Step 3: Upload scanned copies of mark sheets, entrance scorecard, and photo ID.
Step 4: Pay the non-refundable application fee of INR 1,000.
Step 5: Appear for a counseling session (online or in-person) based on merit rank.
Step 6: Confirm seat allotment by paying the admission confirmation fee within 7 days.

4. IMPORTANT DATES
Application window opens: May 1, 2026
Application deadline: June 20, 2026
Entrance counseling rounds: July 1 - July 15, 2026
Classes commence: August 1, 2026

5. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
10th and 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, migration certificate, entrance
scorecard, category certificate (if applicable), passport-size photographs, and
Aadhaar card or equivalent government ID.
