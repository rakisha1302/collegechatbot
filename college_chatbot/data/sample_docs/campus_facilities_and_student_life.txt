SPRINGFIELD INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY - CAMPUS FACILITIES & STUDENT LIFE

1. HOSTEL FACILITIES
Separate hostels are available for men and women, each with 24/7 security,
Wi-Fi, laundry service, and a common recreation room. Rooms are allotted on a
double/triple-sharing basis. Applications for hostel accommodation open along
with admission confirmation and are allotted on a first-come, first-served
basis subject to availability.

2. TRANSPORTATION
The institute operates a fleet of buses covering 12 routes across the city.
Bus passes must be renewed each semester through the transport office. Route
maps and timings are published on the student portal.

3. CAFETERIA
The main cafeteria and two satellite food courts operate from 7:30 AM to 9:00
PM daily, offering a mix of vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and health-focused meal
options. Meal plans can be purchased monthly or per semester.

4. LIBRARY SERVICES
The central library holds over 80,000 volumes, subscriptions to major IEEE and
ACM digital libraries, and a 24-hour reading room during examination periods.
Students can reserve books online and request inter-library loans.

5. PLACEMENT CELL AND INTERNSHIPS
The placement cell coordinates campus recruitment drives with over 150
recruiting companies annually, along with a mandatory 8-week internship
program in the 6th semester. Pre-placement training includes aptitude, coding,
and communication skill workshops starting from the 5th semester.

6. STUDENT CLUBS AND CULTURAL EVENTS
Active student clubs include the Coding Club, Robotics Club, Entrepreneurship
Cell, Literary Society, and the Music & Dance Club. The annual technical
festival "TechVista" and cultural festival "Spring Fiesta" are flagship events
each year.

7. NSS AND NCC
The National Service Scheme (NSS) and National Cadet Corps (NCC) units conduct
regular community service camps, blood donation drives, and leadership
training programs. Enrollment is open at the start of every academic year.

8. SPORTS FACILITIES
Facilities include a football ground, basketball and volleyball courts, an
indoor badminton hall, a gymnasium, and a swimming pool. The institute
participates in inter-collegiate tournaments across various sports.
