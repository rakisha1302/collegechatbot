SPRINGFIELD INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY - FEE STRUCTURE & SCHOLARSHIPS 2026-27

1. TUITION FEES (PER ANNUM, IN INR)
B.Tech (all branches except AI & DS): INR 1,20,000
B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Data Science: INR 1,40,000
M.Tech (all branches): INR 90,000
MBA: INR 1,50,000

2. OTHER FEES
Hostel fee (per annum, including mess): INR 85,000
Transportation fee (per annum, optional): INR 25,000
Laboratory and library fee (per annum): INR 10,000
Examination fee (per semester): INR 2,500

3. SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE
Merit Scholarship: 50% tuition fee waiver for students scoring above 95th
percentile in the entrance examination.
Sports Scholarship: Up to 30% tuition fee waiver for state/national level
sports achievers, subject to trial selection.
Need-Based Scholarship: Up to 40% fee waiver for students with verified family
income below INR 3,00,000 per annum.
Sibling Discount: 10% tuition fee discount for the second sibling enrolled
concurrently.

4. HOW TO APPLY FOR SCHOLARSHIPS
Submit the scholarship application form available on the student portal along
with supporting documents (income certificate, sports certificates, or mark
sheets) within 30 days of admission confirmation. The scholarship committee
reviews applications monthly and disburses awards as fee credits in the
following semester.

5. PAYMENT MODES
Fees can be paid via net banking, UPI, or demand draft in favor of "Springfield
Institute of Technology". Installment payment (two installments per semester)
is available on request through the accounts office.

6. REFUND POLICY
Tuition fee refunds follow the state government's regulatory refund schedule:
100% refund if withdrawal is requested before classes commence, reducing on a
sliding scale thereafter. No refund is issued after 90 days from the start of
the semester.
