SPRINGFIELD INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY - ACADEMIC CALENDAR & EXAMINATION POLICIES

1. SEMESTER SCHEDULE
Odd Semester: August 1 - December 15
Even Semester: January 5 - May 20
Winter Break: December 16 - January 4
Summer Break: May 21 - July 31

2. ATTENDANCE POLICY
A minimum of 75% attendance in each subject is mandatory to be eligible to sit
for the end-semester examination. Students with attendance between 65-74% may
apply for condonation with a valid medical certificate, subject to approval by
the Dean of Academics. Students below 65% attendance are detained and must
repeat the semester.

3. EXAMINATION TIMETABLE POLICY
Internal assessments (2 per semester) are scheduled in the 6th and 12th week of
each semester. End-semester examinations begin two weeks after the last
teaching day. The detailed timetable is published on the student portal at
least 3 weeks before the examination start date.

4. GRADING SYSTEM
The institute follows a 10-point Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) system.
Internal assessment carries 30% weightage and the end-semester exam carries
70% weightage toward the final grade in each subject.

5. SYLLABUS AND CURRICULUM
The curriculum is revised every 4 years in line with the state technical
university's model curriculum and industry inputs from the Board of Studies.
Detailed subject-wise syllabi are available on the academics section of the
student portal.

6. LABORATORY FACILITIES
Each department maintains dedicated laboratories: programming labs, digital
electronics labs, mechanical workshops, materials testing labs, and a central
research lab equipped with high-performance computing clusters for AI/ML
project work.

7. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
Academic grievances (regarding grades, attendance, or examination issues)
should be submitted in writing to the respective Head of Department within 7
days of the issue arising. Unresolved grievances are escalated to the Academic
Grievance Redressal Committee, which convenes monthly.
